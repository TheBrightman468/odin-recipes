Creamy Carbonara

Description

This Creamy Carbonara recipe is a plate of heavenly, creamy pasta. Silky spaghetti with crispy pancetta in a super creamy and cheesy sauce.

Simply delicious and so easy to make at home!

Ingredients

  • Pruscuitto
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Cream
  • Pasta
  • Pepper & Basil

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Saute the pruscuitto
  3. Mix eggs and cheese
  4. Combine the pasta with the meat and sauce
